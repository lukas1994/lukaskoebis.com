Making a Music-Synced AI Film of Grieg's Mountain King

Lukas Koebis · July 19, 2026 · fal × Sequoia 72h Video Hackathon

Over the weekend, for the fal × Sequoia 72h Video Hackathon, I built a short AI film set to Edvard Grieg's In the Hall of the Mountain King.

Story

A trespasser enters a living mineral hall. As the theme repeats and speeds up, the cave wakes: walls breathe, stone ripples, crystal corridors rearrange. In the climax, he is pulled into the rock itself — and the mountain was the creature all along.

Process

  • Analyzed the music (tempo, onsets, bars, percussion hits, and accelerando), then built an adaptive beat grid so every cut and transition is locked to the music.
  • Style lock with stills first (realistic materials + one impossible twist per shot).
  • Keyframes in Seedream 5.0 Pro.
  • Animation in Seedance 2.0.
  • Continuity via handoff frames (last frame of one shot → first frame of the next).
  • Character workaround for likeness blocks: bake identity into a single still, then animate only that image.
